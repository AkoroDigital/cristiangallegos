Who I Am

More Than a Haircut

My name is Cristian, and I'm a barber based in New York. I got into barbering because I've always been drawn to the culture: the community, the craft, and the way a great cut can genuinely change how someone carries themselves. Every client who sits in my chair deserves a clean, precise cut and a comfortable experience from start to finish.

My Story

I started cutting because barber culture spoke to me — the tradition, the skill, and the confidence it gives people walking out the door. What began as a passion quickly became a craft I take seriously every single day. I'm always learning, always refining, and always pushing to be better for the people who trust me with their look.

My Promise

I keep my work clean and make sure every client has a good experience — not just a good cut. Whether it's your first time in my chair or you've been coming for years, I want you leaving with a cut you actually feel great about. No rushed appointments, no half-measures. Just solid work and real attention to detail.
